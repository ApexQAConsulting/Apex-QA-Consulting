Privacy Policy

At Apex QA Consulting ("we," "us," "our"), we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, store, use, and disclose your personal information in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of information about you: personal information such as name, address, email; business-related information such as company name, size, business type; technical information such as IP address, browser type, operating system; and usage data such as how you use our website, products, or services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide our services, respond to inquiries, manage our relationship with you, improve our website, and fulfill any contractual obligations we may have with you. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your personal data and prevent its loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the GDPR, you have various rights in relation to your personal data. These include the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the use of your personal data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at our contact details below.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the contact information provided by you or through notice on our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide our services, respond to inquiries, manage our relationship with you, improve our website, and fulfill any contractual obligations we may have with you.
